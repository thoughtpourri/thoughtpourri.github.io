Why thoughtpourri.

thoughtpourris carry the nostalgia of typewritten letters and add a vintage touch to your words.
With personalised illustrations of various styles you can choose from, we make sure that no two thoughtpourris are ever alike.
Let someone know how much they mean to you. Instead of picking up a gift off the shelf, send them a one-of-a-kind thoughtpourri!

What can be a thoughtpourri?

At the moment, we accept messages in the form of letters. thoughtpourris are crafted such that your letter fits on one side of an A4 page, so make sure it fits otherwise we'd have to use another sheet and that'd create incontinuity. It's completely fine if you exceed a sheet, but just so you know :)

How long will my thoughtpourri take?

Each illustration on a thoughtpourri, right from conceptualisation to final touches, is made in a unique and personalised way. So, one thoughtpourri will take at least 7 days to make, so place your order a week or two in advance. We request you to oblige if we have a lot of thoughtpourris on buffer. Every thoughtpourri is crafted with the same love. One love.

What's the future for thoughtpourri?

We did start out as a fun project to keep ourselves occupied for the summer of '16 but we're definitely not gonna stop there. We plan on expanding into postcards and more other personalised goodies that combine the best of both our worlds, classy and artistic.
On a more serious note, we're trying to build thoughtpourri. into a social brand by giving jobs to the typists who are depleting in number due to the advancements in technology and giving more opportunities to artists and designers and build a community that'll make thoughtpourri. an on-demand service, anywhere.
